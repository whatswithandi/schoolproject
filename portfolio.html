Who am I?

As someone who takes photos and directs the artistic vision behind them, I combine technical skill with creative direction. With my camera, I capture moments, but it's more than just that. I don't just aim and shoot—I plan, design, and execute. I frame the perfect shot and then enhance it with creative direction, ensuring each photo tells a story. It's a dual role where I use my technical know-how in photography alongside my creative instincts in directing to craft images that are not just visually appealing but also emotionally impactful.

What am I doing?

Photography

My approach to photography transcends conventional boundaries. I delve deep into the interplay of light, shadow, and composition, sculpting moments into visual poetry.
Experimentation is my playground—I explore unconventional angles, distort reality, and manipulate elements to craft thought-provoking imagery.

Art Direction

My approach to art direction is about crafting compelling visual narratives. I curate every aspect of a scene—colors, textures, and visual elements—to add depth and meaning to the image. I bring concepts to life by merging different ideas, aiming to create a captivating and immersive experience. My direction isn't just about what's visible but also about evoking emotions and connections, making the visual story resonate on a deeper level.
